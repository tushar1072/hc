FAQ

Common Questions

What geographical locations do you cover?
We provide physical, on-site setup advisory services across India. We have completed over 200 launches in Chandigarh, Punjab, Himachal Pradesh, Udaipur, Delhi-NCR, Bengaluru, Pune, and beyond.
How are consultancy fees calculated?
Our charges are milestone-based, determined by the size and complexity of your project. We offer customized scopes ranging from modular services (kitchen CAD planning only) to turnkey launches.
Do you assist with commercial licensing?
Yes, we provide end-to-end advisory on standard licensing required by hotels and restaurants in India, helping compile dossiers for FSSAI, local Health Trade licenses, Excise, and Fire NOCs.
Are we required to procure equipment through specific vendors?
Not at all. We provide comprehensive layout drawings and equipment specs. We help gather quotes from multiple top fabricators across India and compare them for you, ensuring you get the best deal.
