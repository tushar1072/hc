Our Founder

Bridging Culinary Art & Unit Economics

"For many years, I worked inside beautiful kitchens in luxury properties, serving thousands of guests. But I realized that outside 5-star hotels, over 70% of independent restaurants and café startups fail in their first 18 months. Not because the food was poor, but because the owners lacked standard inventory tools, standardized kitchen blueprints, and professional recipe costs control templates."

Arjun Mehta established Élite Hospitality Consultants with a single goal: bringing 5-star system design and execution rigor to independent entrepreneurs across India.

Since then, Arjun has personally led over 200 hospitality setups in 18 states, helping café chains, hotel investors, and kitchen networks open on time and maximize unit profit margins.
