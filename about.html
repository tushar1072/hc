Our Story & Philosophy

Who We Are

Building Sustainable Legacies

Founded by Chef Arjun Mehta after two decades in 5-star operations and restaurant setup roles, Élite Hospitality Consultants was born out of a simple realization: many hospitality ventures fail not due to poor food, but due to lack of planning, faulty layout ergonomics, and unmonitored food costing systems.

Since our inception in Chandigarh, we have scaled to become India's leading specialized advisory team. We combine culinary creativity with strict financial controls, ensuring our clients build brand empires that are highly profitable from day one.

Methodology

Our Proven Framework

A structured four-phase transition from initial site selection to grand opening and post-launch audits.

01

Phase 1: Discovery & Strategy

Feasibility analysis, local competitor audits, concept creation, financial modeling, and licensing roadmap definition.

02

Phase 2: Planning & Design

Kitchen CAD drafting, MEP layout blueprints, menu creation, recipe standardization, and kitchen equipment vendor sourcing.

03

Phase 3: Execution & Training

Staff recruitment campaigns, detailed training programs for kitchen/service teams, SOP drafting, and dry-run dinners.
